About Us

At Digital Green, we are driving change by building on the social networks of the world’s most vulnerable. We use an innovative digital platform that brings together technology with global development efforts to enable everyone to live a life of dignity. We began as a research project in Microsoft Research India’s Technology for Emerging Markets team in 2006, and spun off as an independent organization in 2008.
