Who we are

We are an international not-for-profit organization, using an innovative digital platform for community engagement to improve livelihoods of rural communities across South Asia and Sub-Saharan Africa.

Know more about us

What we do

We engage with and empower rural communities to produce participatory localized videos on improved agricultural, livelihood, health, and nutrition practices and disseminate these videos through human mediation. These videos are of the community, by the community and for the community.

Know more about our work

Who we work with

Our partners include prominent public, private and civil society organizations in the fields of rural development, research and technology. The Ministry of Rural Development (Government of India), Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, and Department for International Development are some of our key supporters.
