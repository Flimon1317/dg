Who we are

We are an international not-for-profit organization, using an innovative digital platform for community engagement to improve livelihoods of rural communities across South Asia and Sub-Saharan Africa.

About us

What we do

We engage with and empower rural communities to produce participatory videos on improved livelihood, health, and nutrition practices and disseminate these videos through human mediation.
