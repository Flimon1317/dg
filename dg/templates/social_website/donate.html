At Digital Green, we strive to help spread the knowledge of new and innovative techniques to smallholder and marginalized farming communities for their improved livelihood and well being.

You can help support us to widen our reach and impact more lives. Your contribution could help equip one of our several rural teams across India and Sub-Saharan Africa with the requisite technology to document and share best practices.

We are a registered 501(c)3 non-profit in the U.S. a Section 12A/80G charitable trust in India.

All donations are tax-deductible. We use PayPal so you can be assured that your contribution by credit card, debit card, or check will be processed safely.

If you need any further information, please contact us at: contact@digitalgreen.org.
